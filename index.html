Who Are We?

We are a non-profit organization who developed an easy way for schools and their communities to work together to make sure students who need assistance affording school lunches are able to do so. All it takes it $5 donation to help provide a lunch to those who need it. No student deserves be hungry!

How It works

Schools that want to participate can sign up and create a profile. School account holders then setup their dashboard with the number of lunches that are needed for their students for the week

Donors can access the list of schools with the number of lunches needed for their students and can choose the school(s) to which they'd like to donate. There is no need to signup if donating, however, donors can create a profile if they'd like to keep track of their donations

Once a donation has been to submitted and completed, the receiving school(s) will receive a notification via email and dashboard of how many lunches were donated and by whom (if applicable). The funds from the donations are then transferred to an approved school funding account to purchase each lunch.
